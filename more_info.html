Exercises:

Beginner: Yoga, Walking, Bodyweight exercises (Squats, Push-ups)

Intermediate: Light resistance training, Swimming, Cycling

Expert: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training), Weightlifting, Plyometrics

Healthy Foods

Eat nutrient-rich, calorie-dense foods like nuts, seeds, lean proteins, and whole grains. Incorporate frequent meals and snacks to increase calorie intake while focusing on balanced nutrition.

See More

Foods:

Underweight: Increase calorie-dense foods like nuts, seeds, whole grains, lean proteins (chicken, eggs, tofu).

Overweight: Focus on balanced meals with vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains. Limit processed foods and sugars.

Obesity Classes: Prioritize nutrient-dense, low-calorie foods like leafy greens, lean proteins, whole grains, legumes.

Lifestyle Tips

Focus on gaining muscle mass through resistance training, avoid skipping meals, manage stress, and ensure sufficient sleep for recovery.

Lifestyle Tips:

Underweight: Focus on muscle gain, avoid skipping meals, manage stress, ensure sufficient sleep.

Overweight: Incorporate daily physical activity, practice mindful eating, set realistic goals.

Obesity Classes: Structured eating plans, gradual weight loss, stress-reducing activities, improve sleep hygiene.
